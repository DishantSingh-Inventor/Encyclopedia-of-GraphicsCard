Verdict

The RX 7800 XT is AMD's answer to the mid-range segment and a very solid 1440p gaming card. It uses the Navi 32 die, which is a smaller chiplet design compared to Navi 31. The 16GB GDDR6 with 64MB Infinity Cache helps compensate for the 256-bit bus. Performance sits right around the RTX 4070 in most games at 1440p, often trading blows. The 263W TDP is manageable, and 16GB VRAM at this price point is genuinely useful for modern games. A well-rounded card for the 1440p gamer who doesn't need ray tracing excellence.
