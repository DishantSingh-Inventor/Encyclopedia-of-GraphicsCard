Verdict

The RTX 4060 Ti 16GB is a mid-range card with a controversial trade-off. It uses the small AD106 die with 4352 CUDA cores and features a very narrow 128-bit memory bus, which limits bandwidth to 288 GB/s — that's the main criticism. The saving grace is 32MB of L2 cache that helps offset the thin bus. At 165W it's very efficient, and DLSS 3 with Frame Generation makes it punch above its weight in supported titles. Good for 1080p enthusiast and 1440p mainstream gaming. The low power draw is genuinely useful for smaller builds and cheaper PSUs.
