Verdict

The RTX 4080 Super is the revised version of the non-Super 4080, bringing more CUDA cores and slightly better value. Using the AD103 die with 10240 CUDA cores, it delivers 4K gaming performance that's roughly 15-20% behind the 4090 but at a significantly lower price point. The 736 GB/s bandwidth on 16GB GDDR6X is excellent. The 4080 Super is what the original 4080 should have been. If you want near-flagship performance without the 4090's price tag or power draw, this is the card.
