Verdict

The RTX 4070 Ti Super hits a real sweet spot. Bumped up from 7680 CUDA cores (non-Super 4070 Ti) to 8448, and crucially the memory bus goes from 192-bit to 256-bit with 16GB GDDR6X. That extra memory bandwidth at 672 GB/s makes a real difference in bandwidth-heavy workloads. It's a seriously fast 1440p card and very capable at 4K. At $799 it competes hard with the RX 7900 XTX in many games, and has better ray tracing and DLSS 3 to boot. The 285W TDP is reasonable for the performance level.
