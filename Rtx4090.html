Verdict

The RTX 4090 is NVIDIA's absolute best consumer GPU. It completely dominates everything — 4K gaming, 8K gaming, AI workloads, content creation. The 76.3 billion transistor AD102 chip with 16384 CUDA cores gives it a performance lead of anywhere from 50% to 90% over the previous gen flagship RTX 3090 Ti. The 1008 GB/s memory bandwidth thanks to 24GB of GDDR6X on a 384-bit bus is essentially untouchable. The trade-off is a 450W TDP and a very large physical card — it's a triple slot design. At $1599 MSRP it's also clearly a luxury purchase, but it is genuinely in a class of its own.
