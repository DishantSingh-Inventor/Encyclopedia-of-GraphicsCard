Verdict

AMD's crown jewel of RDNA 3. The RX 7900 XTX is the first chiplet-based consumer GPU from AMD, using a 5nm Graphics Compute Die (GCD) paired with multiple 6nm Memory Cache Dies (MCDs). At $999 it goes toe-to-toe with the RTX 4080 Super in rasterization performance. The 24GB GDDR6 at 960 GB/s bandwidth and 96MB Infinity Cache is a massive advantage in high-res, VRAM-hungry scenarios. Rasterization performance is exceptional — some games it beats the 4080 Super. Where it falls short is ray tracing (weaker RT hardware than NVIDIA) and no equivalent to DLSS 3 Frame Generation at launch. Still, one of the best AMD cards ever made.
